I don't have any recommendations in particular but the cutters from china are getting cheap, I had a look on aliexpress, there are a couple for <$1k and more capable ones sitting between $2-5k. You want at least 40w. Thanks to the surge of DIY 3d printers, the motion controls can be upgraded to something with a large working body of knowledge to repair/improve them, if you get stuck, it's possible to swap out the electronics. Here's a [blog post](http://3dprintzothar.blogspot.ca/2014/08/40-watt-chinese-co2-laser-upgrade-with.html) I found of someone doing just that.
