"An US person could be liable for corruption charges in the US for paying a bribe in a foreign country, because of [FCPA](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Foreign_Corrupt_Practices_Act). Quoting: ""The anti-bribery provisions of the FCPA make it unlawful for a U.S. person [..] to make a payment to a foreign official for the purpose of obtaining or retaining business for or with, or directing business to, any person."""
