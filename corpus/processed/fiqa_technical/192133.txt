See RBI regulation Persons Resident in India borrowing in INR from NRIs/PIOs 2.1 Available routes for borrowing: Persons resident in Indian may borrow in INR from NRIs/PIOs under the following two routes: 2.1.1 Borrowing in INR by persons other than companies in India: A person resident in India, not being a company incorporated in India, may borrow in INR from NRIs/PIOs after satisfying the following terms and conditions: Borrowing shall be only on a non- repatriation basis; The amount of loan should be received either by inward remittance from outside India or by debit to NRE/NRO/FCNR(B)/NRNR/NRSR account of the lender, maintained with an authorised dealer or an authorised bank in India; Period of loan shall not exceed 3 years; Rate of interest on the loan shall not be more than two per cent above Bank Rate prevailing on the date of availment of loan; Payment of interest and repayment of principal shall be made only to the NRO account of the lender.
