"Suggest concretely writing out the 3-5 traits you're looking for, gaps you're looking to fill. Assign an acronym to each bullet. When you plan out your questions, write the acronym of the bullet you're looking to assess next to each Q. If none fit, you should seriously consider why you're asking the question. Here's some examples I used for a sales-related search: ""(PO) Process Orientation - Understands the anatomy of sales, stage breakdown and discrete steps in a sales process. (CR) Creativity - Doesn't need a well established playbook of scripts, processes to make deals happen. .."""
