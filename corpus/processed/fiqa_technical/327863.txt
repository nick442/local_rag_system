The definitive answer -- if you want to call it that -- is in Title 26, Section 529(e)(5): (5) Eligible educational institution The term “eligible educational institution” means an institution- (A) which is described in section 481 of the Higher Education Act of 1965 (20 U.S.C. 1088), as in effect on the date of the enactment of this paragraph, and (B) which is eligible to participate in a program under title IV of such Act.
