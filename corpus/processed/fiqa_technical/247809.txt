Social Media has evolved and matured rapidly over the last 2-3 years and today is developing the way businesses are marketing their products and services to a wide yet highly selected viewers of online users. The early days of social media and the limited capabilities of platforms such as Facebook, LinkedIn, Tumblr, Pinterest, and Twitter meant that brands focused their social media marketing in dubai efforts around customer service and customer communication. While these elements are still extremely important, the platforms now allow targeted communication via text and video that bring businesses closer than ever to both existing and potential customers.
