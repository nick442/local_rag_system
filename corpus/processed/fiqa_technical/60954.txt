"the auto industry maybe has to worry about safety standards, however these standards have been set for decades. It's not like they're making new ""kinds"" of cars that would be unsafe by adopting a completely different design or principle. But for drugs, every mechanism in health is different. The company would then have to pay a settlement, such as [Merck and Vioxx](http://www.npr.org/templates/story/story.php?storyId=5470430) resulting in a ~$5 billion loss.
