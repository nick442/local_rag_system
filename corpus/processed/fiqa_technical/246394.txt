While disputes exist about how to define poverty, the poverty rate in India appears to have been on the decline for years in both percentages and raw numbers. ([source](https://blogs.wsj.com/indiarealtime/2013/07/25/how-to-read-indias-poverty-stats/)) It was thought by the end of the 20th century India would hit the Malthusian wall and starve to death. However, human ingenuity, being limitless, held that problem at bay. Now, India's official poverty rate is much closer to America's and the economy is growing at over 7% a year.
