Same thing happened in the college degree market. When fewer people were obtaining degrees a degree nearly guaranteed a better paying future. But as more and more people go after degrees the price has skyrocketed while the pay has not. In fact many positions that didn't used to even require a degree are now requiring degrees without any more pay than what used to get paid for the non-degree employee.
