There goes that myth again, that Ford paid his workers well out of the goodness of his heart. [This article](http://www.forbes.com/sites/timworstall/2012/03/04/the-story-of-henry-fords-5-a-day-wages-its-not-what-you-think/) looks into the actual reason early Ford workers were paid well, which was primarily to cut the massive turnover rate of overworked employees, and the costs of retraining new hires.
