The top 3, and 5 of the top 10, H1-B sponsors are [India-based outsourced IT companies](http://www.business-standard.com/article/companies/here-are-the-25-biggest-h1b-sponsors-of-2016-117013101018_1.html). The core business model of these companies is to displace employees, typically Americans, with much cheaper labor. Their claims that they can't find qualified US workers (an essential part of the H1-B process) are patently fraudulent, as they could hire the people whose jobs they eliminated. These companies undeniably depress IT salaries.
