One of the easiest mistakes to make in business is assuming that your own anecdotal experience applies to everyone on earth. It doesn't, you'd be surprised how different people can be sometimes. People do see and click on ads, you just don't belong to this group, but it's very much alive. And while Ad Blocker usage is increasing it pales in comparison to the amount of people using the internet on a regular basis; it will always represent a small percentage of the internet population.
