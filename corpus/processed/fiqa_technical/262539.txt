Many in this thread appear to have little knowledge about the history of anti-trust law in the US. 50 years ago it would be inconceivable for any one firm to have as much market dominance as any of these 3. We need a rethinking of anti-trust law that properly addresses internet monopolies. If you haven't read it yet, I highly recommend this paper by Lina Khan on Amazon's market dominance and anti trust: https://www.yalelawjournal.org/pdf/e.710.Khan.805_zuvfyyeh.pdf Barry Lynn and the Open Markets Institute are doing great research here as well.
