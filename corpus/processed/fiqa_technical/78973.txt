Imaging driving a bicycle turned backwards, steering it with the steering wheel in the back, while trying to keep to the center of the road. Just keeping going down the road requires a huge degree of control, and constant small adjustments.. If you get it trouble, and turn the wheel in the wrong direction, the trouble increases exponentially, ending in a huge crash. Now the swirling road is futures market, the bicycle is your account, turning the steering wheel left and right is going long or short futures, with steering wheel angle being your position size.
