The Indian Scout motorcycle was popular in all over the world from 1920 until 1939. Indian 101 Scout motorcycles were used by police, motorcycle racers, and during the second World War. During the war, Indian motorcycle has best engines, motors for boats, air conditioners, and bicycles.
