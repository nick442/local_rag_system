"**Chaebol** A chaebol ( TCHAY-bol; JEH-bəl; from Korean jaebeol [tɕɛ̝.bʌl]) is a South Korean form of business conglomerate. They are typically global multinationals and own numerous international enterprises, controlled by a chairman with power over all the operations. The term is often used in a context similar to that of the English word ""conglomerate"".
