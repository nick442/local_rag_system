First of all, do not let your balance carry over and build interest - always pay off the balance at the end of the month. Second, do not buy more than you could afford if you were using a Debit Card or Cash - make purchases you know you can afford. As for where to use your credit card, in practical terms if you're following the two statements above to the letter, anywhere you would be able to use a Debit Card you can use your Credit Card instead - it doesn't have a huge impact on your credit score, but it does give you a credit identity at the very least.
