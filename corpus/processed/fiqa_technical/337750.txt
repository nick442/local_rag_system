Russ Krivor Russ and Igor Krivor are brothers who started DLC Residential in 2005. As early as 2004, the Krivor brothers noticed that there was a growing need for single-family homes in Miami, Florida. This knowledge quickly turned into a business as they began to help investors grow their wealth through real estate. Igor and Russ Krivor change their business model based on what is most likely to yield real returns for their investors.
