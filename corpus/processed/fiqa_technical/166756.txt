In outdoor settings, stainless steel balustrades are washed off clean by the rains leading to almost zero maintenance.The polished look of Steel Balustrades gives your home a sleek and stylish appearance. They are a practical option when it comes to the safety of the users due to their strength and durability. Solid stainless steel is heavy and may be difficult to install but tubular stainless steel makes the balustrade lightweight and easier to handle.
