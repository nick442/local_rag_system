The notion of 'most common' is an interesting one. US citizens have gotten heavier and many have gotten taller. There is more of an emphasis on physical fitness in the military now than in previous generations. Needless to say, the addition of women to the force has been significant in this regard. All of which suggests that in addition to gruehunter's apt comment below, a regular updating of what 'most common' means may lead one to conclude that what used to fit that description no longer does.
