SLA's or Service Level Agreements, [Wikipedia Link](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Service-level_agreement) for business-class internet will have very high availability, and very short time-to-restoration. Whereas, at your home, if a DSL or Coax line gets damaged between the CO and your home, it may take upwards of several days to a week before someone will repair the line. You're willing to pay less for home internet with the risk that you will have a multi-day outage. Businesses can lose tens of thousands to tens of millions of dollars of productivity if their internet connections goes dark.
