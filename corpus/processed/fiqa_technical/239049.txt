Lightning rod has been protecting places for 100 of years. This is a tried and tested methods that has been used by most of the worlds tall buildings. Only the technology on which it is built has advanced as per the modern requirements. A lightning rod is erected on the roof of a building and it is connected to a conducting channel to take the huge discharge of electricity into the ground.
