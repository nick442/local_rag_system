In general, you can do whatever you'd like with the money. Many (perhaps most?) banks offer different ways to receive the money, with the two most popular options being direct payment to other debts, or direct deposit into your bank account. If you choose the latter you can do whatever you'd like with that money once you receive it. Even in the case when you have the bank make direct payments to other credit cards I'm guessing they will not care if it's someone else's name on the account, but that could be bank specific.
