Within the U.K., Third Party Resellers establish warehouses in the UK, but orders are placed overseas, and Amazon is not required to collect Value Added Tax on behalf of allegedly foreign owned resellers which rely on Amazon to sell items. Amazon also evades the payment of tax by routing all sales through Luxembourg which, thanks to Jean Claude Juncker of the EU when Prime Minister of Luxembourg negotiated a generous tax sweetheart deal for multi national corporations (incl Amazon) to establish their base in Luxembourg.
