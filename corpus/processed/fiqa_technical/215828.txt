You can literally see the bloom coming off Amazon, Google, Facebook, Uber and silicon valley in general. Post industrial revolution; it is cyclical and almost predictable. At some point, society collectively goes WTF have we allowed to happen? * Trust busting the Railroads, Robber barons and commodity cartels * Post-depression regulation of financial firms and collective investment schemes * AT&T break-up of the telephone monopoly * Microsoft anti-trust Post-Russia investigation, there will be some attempt to make information dissemination platforms responsible for the veracity of content (at least in paid or promoted political ads). It also seems like Amazon is on a collision course with regulation - especially if they are able to leverage their e-commerce platform into new sectors of the economy. * Note: I'm not posting in favor or against. Just highlighting a trend that appears obvious to me.
