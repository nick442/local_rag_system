Calculate your effective income per day (after taxes) and calculate the prize of everything per day. This lets you easily compare your rent with other (smaller) expenses. It's easy to say that if you stay below your income every day you will save money. It also helps to know how much freely spendable money you have each day (money after taxes, rent, bills etc.). Let the days were your expenses are higher than your income be scarce and know how many days you need to work and save to afford something.
