Healthcare systems are complex and imperfect, there's no single metric. However, there's the [Bloomberg Healthcare Efficiency Index](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2016-09-29/u-s-health-care-system-ranks-as-one-of-the-least-efficient) published annually since 2012 which provides a consistent method over several years. The most recent one shows the US being #50 out of 55 countries. Their methodology and data sources are described at the bottom of the[ linked table](https://assets.bwbx.io/images/users/iqjWHBFdfxIU/iNK1THx4aD20/v3/-1x-1.png).
