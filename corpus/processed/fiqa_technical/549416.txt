RMA Journal is good. You should search for more in depth articles if you really want a good handle on all of the recent regulations. Dodd-Frank, CFPB regs, Basel II and the new capital reserve requirements and how that's affecting the industry. The irony about much of the new regs is that they are driving smaller lenders into selling their company because it is simply too costly to carry the staff necessary to ensure compliance; thus making the too big to fails even bigger.
