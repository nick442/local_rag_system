Tax deductions, credits, and special rates are done for two reasons: Encourage a behavior: Try to lower taxes for the poor/disadvantaged and raise taxes for the rich. Every one of these programs is in essence a loophole that has been established to meet a goal. They are hard to eliminate because some people use them and don't want them to go away. Of course everybody want some program they don't use to be eliminated as long as the rates for everybody else are also lowered.
