"The transfer is easy. Usually you just sign over the title to the new owner, and you're set. Blue book price is a fantasy designed to get you in a car dealership to trade in your car. You won't get ""blue book"" pricing. I would use NADA ""Yellow Book"" or Edmunds TMV pricing as a guide. A dealer won't pay you a good price, but will take the car off your hands quickly. Selling a car privately can be a real pain if you don't have the personality for it. You'll get more money and more hassle. Figure out where people do private party sales in your area. In my area, there's an autotrader magazine that dominates the market. Craigslist is going to attract the types of people who frequent craiglist, which is not always a good thing."
