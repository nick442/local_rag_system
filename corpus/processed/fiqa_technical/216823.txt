Lowered bid ask spread. If you as a market market have to onto inventory (risk) for a long period then you have to charge your customers a commensurate premium. The groups who have lost because of HFT are big banks, any large investor who used to get preferential treatment from brokers, and floor traders. The groups who have won are retail investors and those investors who weren't large or connected enough to get preferential treatment from brokers (majority of investors).
